=Description=
This mod adds Roland's Black Glove from Library of Ruina,
with which you can use various workshop weapons, and the super attack - Furioso.
You can get it with a new player named Roland or Black Silence.
Only your name is "Roland" or "Black Silence",you can use the weapon in the glove.

The mod cannot be used in multiplayer mode.

Thanks to 秋风Pro's work on porting to 1.4 -Volknet_Online

=介绍=
该模组加入了《废墟图书馆》中的罗兰的黑色手套，
佩戴手套可以挥动多种工坊武器以及大招-Furioso。
你可以创建一个名为罗兰或者漆黑噤默的新人物来获得。
只有当你的名字是"罗兰"或"漆黑噤默"时，你才能使用。

该mod无法在多人游戏中使用。
